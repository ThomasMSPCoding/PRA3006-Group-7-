WELCOME TO THE ONCOMAP PROJECT

The OncoMap Project is a student-led initiative from Maastricht University designed to help visualize common cancer locations, understand their symptoms, and learn more about them.

Our goal is to provide an engaging and educational resource that simplifies the complex information surrounding cancer. Whether you're a curious learner or someone seeking to expand their knowledge, the OncoMap Project is here to guide you.
